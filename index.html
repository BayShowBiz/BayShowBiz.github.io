Shows

Choose from three uplifting programs

All shows are 1-hour, designed for AL, IL, MC, and HC with a mix of 40s–80s music or themed sets: Big Band, Country, Broadway, Holiday, and more.

Show 01

Live Instrument Sing-Along

A lively sing-along where Colinda plays several instruments such as ukulele, mandolin, banjolele, electric guitar, small guitars, violin, and steel drum. She brings a minimum of four instruments to each show and guides the group through familiar favorites.

On average, communities enjoy singing together to about 20 songs in this hour-long show.

Show 02

Music, Lights & Dancing

A magical show filled with movement and sparkle. Colinda wears special light-up clothing and moves throughout the room, singing with residents to professional soundtracks. Great for singing along, toe tapping, clapping, and dancing.

Wonderful for larger events, holiday parties, and “something special” on the calendar.

Show 03

Private Room Visits

Gentle, one-on-one or small-group visits for residents who stay in their rooms or are in hospice. With a ukulele and soft songs, Colinda creates calm, meaningful musical moments for residents and their families.

Ideal for hospice care, recovery, and residents who benefit from quieter, intimate music time.

About Colinda

Music, heart, and senior-living experience

A professional entertainer who understands both the joy of music and the needs of senior communities.

Colinda De Groen has been a professional entertainer for over eight years, with four of those years dedicated to senior living and dementia-focused communities. She finds deep joy in bringing music to active seniors, assisted living residents, individuals with dementia, and skilled nursing facilities. Her shows are upbeat, interactive, and heartwarming — with plenty of ShowBiz!

Drawing on her experience working inside senior communities, she understands how to adapt to different care levels, room layouts, and energy levels on any given day. She collaborates easily with Activities Directors to match themes, holidays, and special occasions on the calendar.
